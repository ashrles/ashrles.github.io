Video Denoising Application

As part of the Multimedia Communications Engineering course, I implemented spatial and temporal denoising algorithms in Matlab. The algorithms were combined in one process for optimal video output. The sum of absolute differences and temporal variance were used for feature detection and motion estimation between frames. Non local mean filters and weighted averages were then used to remove noise from the blocks in motion and the still blocks. Finally the blocks were recombined for the final video.
